Bootstrap Container Demo

Below are examples of different .container classes to illustrate how they behave across various breakpoints.

.container

This container has a fixed max-width at each responsive breakpoint (sm, md, lg, xl, xxl).

.container-sm

This is 100% wide until the screen width reaches ≥576px (sm), then it becomes fixed-width.

.container-md

This is 100% wide until ≥768px (md), then becomes fixed-width.

.container-lg

This is 100% wide until ≥992px (lg), then becomes fixed-width.

.container-fluid

This container is always 100% wide, regardless of screen size.
